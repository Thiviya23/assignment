YOUR ROADMAP TO EVENT MANAGEMENT SUCCESS

  1. Earn a Bachelor's degree in New Media Communication
  2. Complete internships with event planning companies
  3. Attend workshops and industry seminars
  4. Gain experience by volunteering at local events
  5. Build a strong professional network and create a portfolio
  6. Obtain certification such as Certified Meeting Professional (CMP)
  7. Work at an event planning firm or start my own business
